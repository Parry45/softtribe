We have managed several software development projects over the years and have been at the forefront of pioneering environmentally friendly, tropically tolerant and locally developed business applications and solutions. Some of these projects that we have implemented include:

  • Revolutionizing the Government of Ghana’s Payroll Systems

  • Developing Ghana’s national identification system

  • Modernizing Utility Billing for the Ghana Water Company Limited

  • Expanding Educational Access to agriculture and agriculture-adjacent students through the development of a bespoke platform produced in collaboration with the Mastercard Foundation for the management of computer inventory, student onboarding, registration, and providing access to a learning portal as well as a community of practice.

  • Working with the Ghana Standards Board to develop standards for the ICT sector to support local organizations to attract global business.

  • Supporting the 13th African Games “Accra 2023” in areas such as development of a bespoke software solution to manage the timing and scoring systems to deliver seamless and accurate results for over 29 sporting disciplines, as well as developing and managing the volunteer management portal.
